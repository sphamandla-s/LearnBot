# LearnBot - WhatsApp Chatbot for Homework and Classwork Assistance

LearnBot is an AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot designed to assist learners with their homework, classwork, and note-taking. It provides a convenient and interactive way for students to get help with their studies directly through WhatsApp.

## Features

- **Homework Assistance**: LearnBot can answer questions, provide explanations, and offer guidance on various subjects to help learners with their homework assignments.

- **Classwork Support**: Learners can ask questions related to their ongoing classwork, concepts taught in class, or any topic they need clarification on.

- **Note Simplification**: LearnBot can simplify complex topics or explanations to make them easier to understand and remember. It can break down concepts into simpler terms or provide concise summaries.
